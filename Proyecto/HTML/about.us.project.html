Our Mission
At Find & Rescue, our mission is simple: to rescue, care for and find loving homes for stray and abandoned animals. Every day, we work tirelessly to give these animals proper attention by providing them with shelter, medical care and a safe enviroment while they wait for their forever home. We believe that every animal deserves love, care and respect. Our team is determined to make a lasting impact by not only saving lives, but also raising awarenessabout the importance of responsable pet ownership. We are working towards creating a compassionate community where no animal is left behind.

Together, we can make a difference, one paw at a time

Get involved!
Wether you're passionate about animal welfare or simply want to make a possitive impact, there are many ways you can get involved. Here's How!:

  • 1. Adopt a pet

    • By adopting a stray or abandoned animal, you not only provide them with a loving home, but also help up free up space for another animal in need. You can check out our adoption page to see our current animals looking for their forever families.

  • 2. Foster a pet

    • As a foster parent, you provide a safe and caring enviroment until the animal is ready for adoption. If you're unable to adopt but still want to help, this might just be the best option for you.

  • 3. Volunteer

    • Wether it's helping at adoption events, walking dogs, or assisting with administrative tasks, your time can make a TON of difference. Volunteering allows us to further spread the word and work more efficiently, we can save more strays by the day!

  • 4. Donate

    • Your generous donations allow us to provide food, medical care, and shelter to the amimals in our care. We also use donations to fund awareness programms and events. Every donation counts, and your contribution, large or small, helps us save lives.
